Why you'll love us

We fit our Best Lifestyle
From a quaint small hotel room to a spacious two-bedroom suite with a private balcony overlooking the water, our individually decorated apartments are completely equipped, so you will feel in the comfort of your home, and even prepare that special dinner to celebrate exciting moments.
Location! Location! Location!
Villa Venezia is a small Boutique Hotel located off Las Olas at a canal - close enough to the bustling town and the beach, but yet tranquil, secluded and romantic. We are located within walking distance from the world-class sandy beaches of Fort Lauderdale, offering the best water sports such as sailing, boating, parasailing, yachting, jet skiing, fishing and more. Also, within a few minutes you are at Las Olas Boulevard, with charming shops, delicious restaurants, great entertainment and awesome nightlife.
